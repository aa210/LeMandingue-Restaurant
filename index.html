ABOUT
Le Mandingue


Le Mandingue is an upscale, ethnic, African restaurant where quality and hospitality meet. Le Mandingue has been a staple ground for both locals and tourists looking for the best African food in the city of Philadelphia for over 12 years. Le Mandingue serves native dishes from across West Arica. We have great food, live music, and a restaurant culture that will leave you with a lasting impression.


MEET THE OWNERS


Mr. Kaba and Fanta, owners of Le Mandingue, are not only trailblazers in the restaurant industry, but are also philanthropists in the African community here and back home.

Before Fanta arrived at becoming the owner of one of the worldâ€™s most successful African restaurants, she faced many obstacles during her journey. Fanta not only came to America as a war victim of Liberia, but she is also an amputee who lost her leg due to a serious car accident. After her accident, tables turned for her, but her favor from God has never changed. In the African community, being an amputee is very rare. At times, Fanta was made to feel like an outcast. However, she kept pushing to follow her dreams to open the infamous Le Mandingue restaurant.

Fanta is an inspiration to African women, people with disabilities, mothers, and small business owners. She takes pride in her five children and her hardworking husband, who has been her backbone throughout her journey. She truly admires her husband because he has never allowed her physical state to interfere with their love. Fanta is a walking model of perseverance.

To have Fanta share her story for press, interviews, or at an event, please email us at Lemandingue1@gmail.com You can catch Mr. Kaba, Fanta, and the rest of the power team working hard 7 days a week at Le Mandingue.
